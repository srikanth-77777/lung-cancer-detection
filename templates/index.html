ℹ️ How does AI explanation work?
Dual Grad-CAM generates two heatmaps: one showing what the AI sees as evidence for its predicted class, and another showing what it would consider evidence for the alternative. When both heatmaps highlight different areas, the model has clearly distinct reasoning. When they overlap, the region is ambiguous.
  • Red heatmap (left) — Regions supporting the predicted diagnosis
  • Green heatmap (right) — Regions the model associates with the alternative class
  • LIME positive — Superpixels that increase the prediction confidence
  • LIME mask — Boundary overlay showing all influential regions
